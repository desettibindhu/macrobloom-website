Nutrition Basics

What your numbers mean

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)

The calories your body needs at rest to support breathing, circulation, and cell maintenance.

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE)

This is your BMR adjusted for activity — the energy needed to maintain your current lifestyle.

Calories & body composition

A calorie deficit promotes fat loss, maintenance preserves weight, and a surplus supports muscle gain.

Personalization matters

These estimates guide your nutrition, but individual needs can vary based on metabolism, stress, and training.
